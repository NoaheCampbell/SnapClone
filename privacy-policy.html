SprintLoop Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2025

1. Information We Collect

SprintLoop collects the following information to provide our study circle services:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and profile details you provide
  • Study Content: Photos, text, and study materials you share in study circles
  • Usage Data: Study session duration, quiz scores, and learning progress
  • Device Information: Push notification tokens to send you reminders and updates

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide study circle functionality and social learning features
  • Generate AI-powered summaries and quizzes to enhance learning
  • Send notifications about study reminders and circle activity
  • Track learning streaks and progress
  • Improve our app and user experience

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only:

  • With other members of your study circles (as intended by the app design)
  • With AI services (OpenAI) to generate study summaries and quizzes
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure with industry-standard encryption. Study materials may have automatic expiration dates for privacy.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your account and associated data at any time
  • Control what you share in study circles
  • Opt out of push notifications in app settings

6. Children's Privacy

SprintLoop is intended for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app.
