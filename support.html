Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a study circle?

Tap the "+" icon in the inbox, enter your circle details, and invite friends with the share code.

How do sprint sessions work?

Start a sprint by choosing your topic and study duration. Take photos of your materials, and SprintLoop will generate AI summaries and quizzes when you finish.

Why can't I see some messages?

Messages in SprintLoop have expiration times for privacy. Check your circle's settings for the current expiry duration.

How do learning streaks work?

Complete study sprints daily to build your streak. Streaks help you stay motivated and track your learning consistency.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account and all associated data from the Settings page in the app.

The app is crashing or not working properly

Try force-closing and reopening the app. If problems persist, email us with your device model and iOS version.
